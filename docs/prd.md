# OpsFlow PRD

## Overview
OpsFlow is a multi-tenant service operations SaaS platform for home service businesses.

## Problem
Small service businesses often manage customers and jobs manually across phone calls, messaging apps, and spreadsheets. This leads to poor visibility, inconsistent workflows, and lack of data tracking.

## Target Users
- Tenant Owner
- Manager
- Staff
- Customer

## MVP Scope
- Authentication (register, login, current user)
- Tenant and team management
- Customer management
- Basic job management (later phase)
- Assignment (later phase)
- Attachments (later phase)
- Notifications (later phase)
- Dashboard (basic metrics)

## Out of Scope (for now)
- AI features
- Payments / billing
- Advanced scheduling
- Mobile apps
- Third-party integrations

## Goal
Build a production-style system demonstrating end-to-end engineering capability.
